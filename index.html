About This Home

Single-Level Living in Barrington

Located in the desirable Barrington subdivision, this brick ranch offers comfortable single-level living with flexible space and strong entertaining potential. The main level features oak hardwood floors throughout the primary living spaces, vaulted ceilings in a versatile living room ideal for a piano room, office, or sitting area, and convenient main-level laundry near the primary suite.

The home offers two potential owner suites, including a private suite off the kitchen and a spacious primary suite with walk-in closet and renovated bathroom featuring a walk-in tile shower and heated towel racks. A guest bedroom features distinctive walnut flooring.

The walkout basement expands the living space with a bar area, theater room with projector and screen, full bathroom, workout space, and additional recreation areas, with potential to add two bedrooms with egress windows. Outdoor living includes a composite deck ideal for gatherings, with a reinforced section wired with a 220V connection and kill switch for a future hot tub.

The oversized 529 sq ft garage with higher doors is complemented by an attached rear storage shed integrated into the roofline, and entry from the garage or front door requires only a few steps.
